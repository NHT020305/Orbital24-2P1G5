USER STORIES

    As students who want to participate in a contest that challenges their knowledge across various fields, we want to create a dedicated platform where they can compete with our peers. There are three kinds of users:

  • Player: Users can enter contest rooms by simply entering a password, without the need for account registration. They can interact with the contest interface using keyboard and mouse inputs, including signaling to the MC.
  • Master of Ceremonies: Responsible for reading questions from the screen during contests, the MC doesn't require any additional actions. In online matches, the MC can also serve as a technician.
  • Technician: This role involves technical tasks such as inputting and uploading questions, selecting questions to display, and managing the countdown timer for answering questions. With the extension feature for server management, technicians will require accounts to access their question banks and initiate matches without affecting other servers.
